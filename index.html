FAQ

Questions About Love

Everything you need to know before starting your journey with Love Matcher.

How is Love Matcher different from regular dating apps?
Unlike swipe-based apps, Love Matcher uses a combination of certified human matchmakers and advanced compatibility algorithms. We vet every member, focus on long-term compatibility, and provide ongoing coaching—not just an endless scroll of profiles.
How long does it typically take to find a match?
Most members receive their first curated match within 7–14 days of joining. The timeline varies based on your plan and preferences. Our Eternal members typically see faster results due to dedicated matchmaker attention.
Is my information kept private?
Absolutely. Your personal information is never shared without your consent. We are fully GDPR compliant and employ bank-level encryption. You control who sees your profile and can adjust visibility settings at any time.
Can I upgrade or change my membership plan?
Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or pause your membership at any time from your account dashboard. Upgrades take effect immediately, and any difference in cost is prorated for the current billing cycle.
What if I don't find a compatible match?
We stand behind our service. If you complete 6 months on our Eternal plan without finding a match, we'll extend your membership at no charge until you do. Your happiness is our mission.
Are your matchmakers certified professionals?
Yes. All Love Matcher matchmakers hold certifications from the Matchmaking Institute or equivalent credentials, and many have advanced degrees in psychology or counseling. They undergo continuous training to stay ahead of relationship science.
